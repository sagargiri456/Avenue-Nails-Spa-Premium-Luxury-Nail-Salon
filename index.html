Our Story of Excellence

Founded in 2015, Avenue Nails and Spa has been Edmonton's premier destination for luxury nail care. Our founders, master nail technicians with over 20 years of combined experience, envisioned a space where artistry meets wellness.

We pride ourselves on creating an oasis of tranquility where every client is treated with personalized attention and care. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond beautiful results to include impeccable hygiene standards and continuous staff training.
